Subscription help

Restore purchases

Open the parent-gated subscription screen and choose Restore Purchases.

Manage or cancel a subscription

Use the in-app Manage Subscription button or go to your Apple ID subscription settings.

Refund requests

Apple manages billing and refunds. Refund requests are generally submitted through Apple support or your Apple purchase history.

Frequently asked questions

Why are some brushes locked?

The free starter tools remain available to everyone. Extra Creator Tools brushes are unlocked with an active subscription.

Can my child purchase a subscription directly?

Subscription entry points are intended to be parent-gated. Parents or guardians should handle purchases, billing, and account settings.

How do I share a drawing?

Export the drawing from the canvas and use the iOS or iPadOS share sheet to choose where to send it.
