Kids Doodle Desk

Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how Sweta Rahul Patel handles information in connection with the Kids Doodle Desk app, including the Creator Tools subscription.

Effective date: March 29, 2026
Kid-focused app StoreKit subscriptions No account required

1. Who we are

Kids Doodle Desk is published by Sweta Rahul Patel. If you have privacy questions, contact us at swetanandha93@gmail.com.

2. What the app does

Kids Doodle Desk is a drawing app for children and families. It offers free drawing tools and optional paid Creator Tools subscriptions that unlock additional brushes and effects.

Parent-gated areas are used for subscription flows, purchase restoration, and subscription management.

3. Information we process

Drawings and exported images

Drawings are created and rendered within the app. If you export a drawing, the app generates an image file and may place that file in temporary device storage so it can be shared using the iOS or iPadOS share sheet.

Subscription and purchase status

Subscription billing is handled by Apple. The app may receive limited transaction and subscription status information from Apple through StoreKit, such as product identifiers, purchase status, entitlement state, and renewal state, so the app can unlock subscribed features and restore purchases.

Support information you send us

If you contact support, we will receive the information you choose to provide, such as your email address, device details, screenshots, and issue description.

4. Information we do not intentionally collect in the current build

  • No in-app account registration.
  • No custom analytics or ad SDKs identified in the current build.
  • No precise location access.
  • No microphone or camera access.
  • No contact list access.
If the app later adds analytics, ads, sign-in, cloud sync, or new permissions, this policy must be updated before release.

5. How we use information

  • To let users create, preview, export, and share drawings.
  • To unlock Creator Tools features for active subscribers.
  • To restore purchases and help parents manage subscriptions.
  • To respond to support requests and fix issues.
  • To comply with legal obligations and protect the app, users, and our rights.

6. How information is shared

We do not sell personal information.

  • Apple: Apple processes subscription payments and provides StoreKit purchase status information.
  • User-selected sharing destinations: If you export and share a drawing, the chosen app or recipient will process that content under their own policies.
  • Legal or safety reasons: We may disclose information if required by law or to protect rights, safety, or the integrity of the service.

7. Data retention

Drawings and export files remain on the device or in temporary device storage until removed by the user or the system. Support emails and related support materials are kept only as long as reasonably needed to respond, troubleshoot, document, or comply with legal obligations.

8. Children and parent controls

Kids Doodle Desk is intended for use by children with parent or guardian involvement. Subscription purchases, restore actions, and legal/support links should be accessed by a parent or guardian.

We do not knowingly ask children to create accounts or submit personal information through the app itself.

9. Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards appropriate to the nature of the app. No method of storage, transmission, or device usage is completely secure.

10. Your choices and rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to request access, correction, deletion, or restriction of personal information we hold. To make a request, contact swetanandha93@gmail.com.

Subscription billing, cancellations, and refund requests are generally handled by Apple through your Apple ID account.

11. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The latest version will be posted at this URL, and the effective date at the top of the page will be updated.
